Prerequisites

Deliberately minimal — this course meets people where they are.

What participants need

  • No coding background. Nothing in the program requires technical skills — vibe coding means Claude writes everything; participants only describe what they want.
  • A laptop and a Claude account (claude.ai).
  • Basic comfort with a browser, email and office documents.
  • 2–3 of their own real business tasks to bring in — the course runs on participants' actual work.
  • For the Autopilot flavor only: a free n8n account (set up together in class).

Free plan or paid?

  • The free plan is enough for meeting 1 — nobody has to pay before they're convinced.
  • From meeting 2 onward, Claude Pro is strongly recommended: higher usage limits, Projects, and larger file uploads — all used heavily in class.
  • A common model: the organization covers Pro for the duration of the course, then decides based on adoption.

How to choose

A simple decision rule for each option.

Pick AI Assistant if…

The pain is personal overload

Participants are drowning in email, meetings and writing. Ten meetings focused entirely on their own workday — two of them just on inbox and meetings.

Pick AI Company if…

You want the full vision

The flagship: dedicated meetings on customers, marketing, hiring and managing workers, operations, vibe coding and AI agents — ending with each participant presenting the AI company they built.

Pick Autopilot if…

The same work repeats every week

Four full meetings of no-code automation and AI agents in n8n. If intake, reports and follow-ups repeat, this ends with a real system running while everyone sleeps.
